Ubud

Ubud

Ubud is the cultural heart of Bali, surrounded by lush jungles, rice terraces, and ancient temples. Known for its serene atmosphere and artistic community, Ubud attracts travelers seeking peace, spirituality, and natural beauty.

The town is filled with art galleries, yoga studios, and traditional markets. Ubud also serves as a gateway to many of Bali’s scenic spots, from waterfalls to monkey forests — offering an authentic and tranquil experience.

Top Culinary Spots in Ubud

Clear Café

A relaxing café offering healthy organic dishes and smoothies in a serene, beautifully designed bamboo setting.

Address: Jl. Hanoman No.8, Ubud

Locavore

An award-winning fine dining restaurant serving modern dishes made from locally sourced ingredients — a must-try experience in Bali.

Address: Jl. Dewi Sita No.10, Ubud

Warung Bintangbali

A local gem overlooking rice fields, offering authentic Indonesian dishes at affordable prices in a relaxed open-air setting.

Address: Jl. Suweta No.108, Ubud

Alchemy

Bali’s first raw vegan café and juice bar, serving fresh salads, smoothie bowls, and raw desserts in a cozy, artistic space.

Address: Jl. Penestanan Kelod No.75, Ubud

Watercress Café

A stylish café serving wholesome brunches, coffee, and international dishes in a bright and lively atmosphere.

Address: Jl. Monkey Forest, Ubud

The Sayan House

Elegant fusion restaurant overlooking the Ayung River Valley — perfect for romantic dining with a breathtaking sunset view.

Address: Jl. Raya Sayan No.70, Ubud
