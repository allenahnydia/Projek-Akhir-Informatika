Nusa Dua

Nusa Dua

Nusa Dua is Bali’s premier resort area, famous for its luxurious hotels, white sandy beaches, and calm turquoise waters. It’s a perfect destination for those seeking comfort, relaxation, and world-class service.

With beautifully landscaped gardens, art galleries, and cultural performances, Nusa Dua combines modern elegance with authentic Balinese charm — making it ideal for families, honeymooners, and leisure travelers.

Top Culinary Spots in Nusa Dua

Kayuputi

Fine dining restaurant at The St. Regis Bali Resort, famous for its elegant coastal views and refined contemporary cuisine using premium local ingredients.

Address: The St. Regis Bali Resort, Kawasan Pariwisata, Nusa Dua, Bali

Bumbu Bali

An authentic Balinese restaurant known for its traditional recipes and cultural ambiance — perfect for experiencing genuine Balinese flavors.

Address: Jl. Pratama, Tanjung Benoa, Nusa Dua, Bali

Soleil at The Mulia

Fine dining beachfront restaurant offering Mediterranean and Pan-Asian dishes with stunning ocean views and luxurious service.

Address: The Mulia Resort, Jl. Raya Nusa Dua Selatan, Nusa Dua

The Pirate’s Bay Bali

A family-friendly, pirate-themed outdoor café with treehouses and sea views — fun for kids and great for groups seeking a unique experience.

Address: The Bay Bali, Nusa Dua Beach, Nusa Dua

Nusa Dua Beach Grill

Casual beachfront dining at Nusa Dua Beach Hotel & Spa offering grilled seafood, cocktails, and relaxed ocean-side vibes — perfect for sunset dinners.

Address: Nusa Dua Beach Hotel & Spa, Nusa Dua, Bali

Nusa Dua Pizza

Casual Italian restaurant famous for its thin-crust pizzas and friendly local vibe — a must-visit for comfort food lovers.

Address: Jl. By Pass Ngurah Rai No.99, Nusa Dua
