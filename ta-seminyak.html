Seminyak Tourist Attractions

Discover the highlights of Bali’s most stylish beach destination.

Welcome to Seminyak

Located just north of Kuta and Legian, Seminyak is known for its luxurious resorts, fine dining, and vibrant nightlife. It’s a place where culture meets comfort from sacred temples to beach clubs and sunset bars. Whether you’re here to relax or explore, Seminyak has something for everyone.

1. Seminyak Beach

Seminyak Beach offers a long stretch of golden sand, calm waves, and some of Bali’s best sunsets. It’s ideal for surfing, beach walks, or simply relaxing at a beachfront bar.

  • Location: Jalan Kayu Aya, Seminyak, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 24 hours (best at sunset)
  • Nearby: Ku De Ta Beach Club, Potato Head, La Plancha
  • Activities: Surfing, beach yoga, cocktail lounges

Tip: Visit during sunset to enjoy colorful skies and chill music at beach bars.

2. Petitenget Temple

Petitenget Temple (Pura Petitenget) is one of the few cultural landmarks in Seminyak, famous for its traditional architecture and serene atmosphere. The temple is still used by locals for ceremonies.

  • Location: Jl. Petitenget No.6, Kerobokan Kelod, Kuta Utara
  • Opening Hours: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Entrance Fee: IDR 20,000
  • Contact: +62 361 9308399

Tip: Wear a sarong and sash (available at the entrance) and visit before sunset for photos.

3. La Plancha Beach Bar

La Plancha is a colorful beachfront bar known for its bean bags, parasols, and Spanish-inspired menu. It’s the ultimate spot to watch the sunset with live music and cocktails.

  • Location: Jl. Mesari Beach, Seminyak, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 10:00 AM – 11:00 PM
  • Contact: +62 878 6141 6310
  • Signature Drink: Tropical Sangria
  • Website: www.laplanchabali.com

Tip: Arrive before 5 PM to get a good spot — it gets packed at sunset!
