Canggu

Canggu

Canggu is a coastal village in Bali known for its relaxed atmosphere, surf beaches, and trendy cafes. It’s a favorite destination for digital nomads, surfers, and travelers seeking a laid-back vibe with modern touches.

Surrounded by rice paddies and ocean views, Canggu offers a perfect blend of traditional Balinese charm and contemporary lifestyle. From beach clubs to organic eateries and yoga studios, this area represents Bali’s youthful and creative energy.

Top Culinary Spots in Canggu

Crate Café

A famous café in Canggu offering smoothie bowls, coffee, and healthy brunches — always full of good vibes and surfers.

Address: Jl. Canggu Padang Linjong No.49, Canggu

Betelnut Café

Healthy and cozy café serving fresh salads, wraps, and smoothie bowls with a view of the rice fields.

Address: Jl. Pantai Batu Bolong No.60, Canggu

The Avocado Factory

Bali’s first avocado-themed café — creative menu featuring everything from avocado toast to smoothies and desserts.

Address: Jl. Tanah Barak No.52, Canggu

Milk & Madu

Family-friendly café offering wood-fired pizzas, burgers, and excellent coffee in a cozy, open-air space.

Address: Jl. Pantai Berawa No.52, Canggu

The Shady Shack

A beloved vegetarian café surrounded by greenery, famous for its vegan burgers and smoothie bowls.

Address: Jl. Tanah Barak No.57, Canggu

Penny Lane

A chic restaurant blending vintage design with Bali’s modern café culture — serving hearty Western and local dishes.

Address: Jl. Munduk Kedungu No.90, Canggu
