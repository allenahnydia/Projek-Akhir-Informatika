Kuta Tourist Attractions

Discover the vibrant energy of Kuta — Bali’s most famous beachfront destination.

Welcome to Kuta

Kuta is Bali’s legendary tourist hub, known for its lively atmosphere, long golden beaches, and endless entertainment options. From surfing to nightlife, Kuta offers an energetic experience for every traveler.

Whether you’re chasing sunsets, shopping by the beach, or exploring local markets, Kuta delivers the perfect mix of adventure and relaxation.

1. Kuta Beach

Kuta Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Bali, famous for its golden sands and vibrant surf culture. It’s ideal for beginner surfers, sunset watchers, and beach lovers alike.

  • Location: Jalan Pantai Kuta, Kuta, Badung, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 24 Hours
  • Entrance Fee: Free
  • Activities: Surfing, beach volleyball, sunset watching, horse riding
  • Nearby: Hard Rock Hotel, Beachwalk Mall, Kuta Art Market

Tip: Rent a surfboard and enjoy the sunset — it’s one of the best in Bali!

2. Waterbom Bali

Waterbom Bali is Asia’s leading water park, offering thrilling slides, lazy rivers, and lush tropical gardens. It’s perfect for families, friends, and anyone looking to cool off under the Bali sun.

  • Location: Jl. Kartika Plaza, Tuban, Kuta
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Entrance Fee: Starting from IDR 350,000
  • Contact: +62 361 755676
  • Website: www.waterbom-bali.com

Tip: Book tickets online to skip the queue and get discounts for groups.

3. Beachwalk Shopping Center

Beachwalk Shopping Center is a modern open-air mall located right across Kuta Beach. It features international brands, restaurants, cinemas, and rooftop views overlooking the ocean.

  • Location: Jl. Pantai Kuta, Kuta, Badung
  • Opening Hours: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  • Contact: +62 361 8464888
  • Website: www.beachwalkbali.com

Tip: Visit in the evening for live music and ocean-view dining experiences.
