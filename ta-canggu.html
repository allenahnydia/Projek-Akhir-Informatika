Canggu Tourist Attractions

Explore Canggu — Bali’s hip coastal village filled with surf, sunsets, and stylish vibes.

Welcome to Canggu

Canggu is Bali’s trendiest coastal town, famous for its black-sand beaches, surf-friendly waves, and vibrant café culture. It’s a favorite destination for digital nomads, surfers, and creative travelers.

Combining bohemian charm and natural beauty, Canggu is perfect for those seeking both relaxation and energy — from sunrise yoga to beach club sunsets.

1. Echo Beach

Echo Beach is one of the most famous surf spots in Bali. With its dramatic black sand, consistent waves, and beachfront bars, it’s a lively hangout for both locals and travelers.

  • Location: Jl. Pura Batu Mejan, Canggu, North Kuta, Badung, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 24 Hours
  • Entrance Fee: Free
  • Activities: Surfing, beachside dining, sunset watching
  • Nearby: La Brisa Bali, Echo Beach Club, Sand Bar

Tip: Visit during sunset for a relaxed beach vibe and amazing photo opportunities.

2. Finns Beach Club

Finns Beach Club is one of the most iconic hangouts in Canggu. With its infinity pools, oceanfront bars, and daily DJ sets, it’s the ultimate spot for daytime relaxation and sunset parties.

  • Location: Jl. Pantai Berawa, Canggu, North Kuta, Badung, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 10:00 AM – 12:00 AM
  • Contact: +62 361 844 6327
  • Website: finnsbeachclub.com

Tip: Book a sunset bed or VIP sofa in advance — the best seats go fast!

3. Tanah Lot Temple

Located about 25 minutes from Canggu, Tanah Lot Temple is one of Bali’s most iconic landmarks. Set dramatically on a rocky offshore formation, it’s a sacred Hindu temple known for breathtaking sunsets.

  • Location: Beraban, Kediri, Tabanan Regency, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM
  • Entrance Fee: IDR 60,000 (foreigners)
  • Contact: +62 361 880361
  • Website: tanahlot.id

Tip: Arrive one hour before sunset for the best lighting and fewer crowds.
