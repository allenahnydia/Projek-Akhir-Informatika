Nusa Dua Tourist Attractions

Discover the elegance of Nusa Dua — where luxury resorts, white-sand beaches, and cultural landmarks meet the calm sea breeze.

Welcome to Nusa Dua

Nusa Dua is Bali’s upscale beach destination, known for its calm waters, pristine beaches, and world-class resorts. It’s the perfect spot for relaxation, fine dining, and family-friendly fun.

With clean, quiet surroundings and beautiful oceanfront views, Nusa Dua represents Bali’s more polished side — ideal for travelers looking for luxury and peace.

1. Nusa Dua Beach

Nusa Dua Beach is famous for its soft white sand, calm waters, and scenic coastline. Ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

  • Location: Nusa Dua, Badung, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Entrance Fee: Free
  • Facilities: Sunbeds, showers, water sports rental

Tip: Perfect for early morning walks and watching the sunrise.

2. Waterblow

Waterblow is a natural phenomenon where waves crash into the limestone cliffs, creating dramatic water sprays that reach high into the air.

  • Location: BTDC Area, Nusa Dua, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Entrance Fee: IDR 25,000

Tip: Visit during low tide for a safer and better viewing experience.

3. Geger Temple

Geger Temple, also known as Pura Geger, is a coastal Hindu temple overlooking the ocean. It's famous for its serene atmosphere and traditional Balinese architecture.

  • Location: Jl. Pantai Geger, Nusa Dua, Bali
  • Opening Hours: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Entrance Fee: IDR 15,000
  • Facilities: Prayer area, scenic viewpoints

Tip: Visit during sunset for stunning ocean views and peaceful vibes.
